Jordan 1 High 85 Black White

Product Details

When it comes to iconic colourways, few silhouettes have as many under their belt as the Air Jordan 1. Amongst some of the first colourways launched in 1985 was a clean and simple black and white version, which has gone on to become one of the most sought-after iterations going. Now, the Air Jordan 1 High ‘85 “Black White” is set to drop in 2023, paying homage to the original ‘85 version through its classic high-cut shape and colour blocking. The “Black White” or “Panda” Jordan 1 High ‘85 comes crafted from smooth, premium leather, and sees a crisp white base give way to black overlays that wrap their way around the silhouette. Nike and Jordan branding hit the tongue tab and ankle flaps respectively, and a black Nike Swoosh sweeps across the shoe’s sidewalls. Elsewhere, a nylon tongue adds a comfortable touch, and a black and white sole unit matches the uppers for a super-clean finish.
